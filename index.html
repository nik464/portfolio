AgriAnalyze — AI Drone Crop Analysis

Intelligent precision agriculture platform leveraging drone imagery and deep learning to democratize crop health monitoring. Farmers upload high-resolution drone photos; AI models instantly detect crop diseases (wheat blast, rice leaf scald, etc.), quantify affected areas, estimate yield impact, and recommend treatment protocols. Built on YOLOv8 object detection trained on 27,000+ labeled agricultural samples, achieving 92% classification accuracy. Generates detailed field health reports with visual annotations, disease severity scores, and treatment timelines — reducing crop loss through early intervention and enabling data-driven farming decisions for smallholder farmers.
